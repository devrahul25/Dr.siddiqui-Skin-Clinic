Decoding Heat-Damaged Hair: Identifying Split Ends, Dryness, and Dullness

08 Dec 2023

We all enjoy experimenting with our hair, whether it involves using styling tools, blow-drying, or occasional visits to the salon. While these styling practices can result in stunning looks, they may also have adverse effects on our cherished locks. If you've observed signs of split ends, dryness, and dullness in your hair, it's worth considering whether your hair has suffered heat damage.

Split Ends: One of the most common indications of heat damage is the presence of split ends. Elevated temperatures from styling tools can lead to the fracturing and splitting of the hair's protective outer layer, known as the cuticle. This results in frayed ends that weaken the hair's structure and increase the risk of breakage.
