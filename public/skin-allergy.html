Skin allergies are most common in people of all ages and across regions.

Irritated skin can be caused by a variety of factors. These include immune system disorders, medications and infections. When the allergens are responsible for triggering an immune system response, then it is an allergic skin condition.

If you’re suffering from a skin allergy, you know it is always skin is dry, flaky skin, rashes on skin and sometimes the itching can be unbearable.

Rashes can also be caused by an illness such as measles or chickenpox. Eczema and hives, both of which are related to allergies, are two of the most common types of skin rashes.
